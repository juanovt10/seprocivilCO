    • Who is Infrastructura y Senalizacion?

    IS is a Colombian company that was founded in 2008. We specialize in providing high-quality services for road signage and other types of road installations. With headquarters located in Villavicencio Meta, IS has become a leader in their field by offering innovative and cost-effective solutions to their clients.

    Over the years, IS has amassed a large portfolio of projects where they have satisfied the needs of public and private entities alike. Their dedication to providing top-notch customer service and their commitment to using the latest technologies have earned them a solid reputation within the industry. Whether it's a small-scale project or a large-scale installation, IS is the go-to choice for those seeking reliable and efficient services.

    Mission

    At Infrastructura y Senalizacion, our mission is to study, design, and manage all types of civil works, whether through contracts, administration, or concessions, for both public and private organizations, with the aim of improving the welfare of society. Innovation is a fundamental part of our corporate culture, enabling us to undertake unique and high-quality projects that leave a distinctive mark on our management and construction approach, reflecting our commitment to the business.

    Vision

    to become a strategic ally for our clients and a highly recognized, profitable company at the national and international level for our creativity and innovation in developing new businesses and fulfilling our commitments. We aim to stand out in providing comprehensive solutions by utilizing state-of-the-art equipment and technology.
